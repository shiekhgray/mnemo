  rewrote the working tree underneath the still-running container (file mtimes matched the push time).
- Fix: `docker compose restart web`. Verified `/mnemo/` and `/mnemo/src/main.jsx` both 200, logs clean.
- Recorded this gotcha in CLAUDE.md.
